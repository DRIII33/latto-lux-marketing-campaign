Hello Team,

I am pleased to report that we have successfully completed Phase 1: Data Collection & Integration for the "Latto Lux" marketing campaign. This critical initial phase establishes the digital backbone for our data-driven strategy and prepares us for the next steps of analysis and optimization.

What We Accomplished:

Established a Central Data Hub: We have successfully set up a dedicated dataset, latto_lux_campaign, within our Google BigQuery project. All future campaign data will be centralized here, providing a single, reliable source for all teams.

Simulated Real-World Data: To prepare for the campaign launch, we generated synthetic data for both physical ticket sales and digital app usage. This allows us to build and test our analytics models and dashboards before the first ticket is ever sold.

Built the Data Bridge: We have created a lttx_combined_view in BigQuery that seamlessly links physical ticket sales to digital engagement on the "Latto Lux" app. This allows us to immediately see how many tickets are being scanned, which features are being used, and which physical retail locations are driving the most digital activity.

What This Means for the Team:

This is a significant milestone. We now have a robust and scalable data pipeline that will enable us to:

Track Performance in Real-Time: We can monitor sales and engagement metrics as they happen.

Make Data-Driven Decisions: We have the foundation to identify our most valuable customers and optimize our marketing spend.

Prove ROI: We can now accurately measure the effectiveness of our campaign elements, from physical sales channels to digital promotions.

Our next steps will focus on Phase 2, which involves Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and the creation of our initial marketing dashboards. We are on track to make this a data-powered campaign that delivers results.
